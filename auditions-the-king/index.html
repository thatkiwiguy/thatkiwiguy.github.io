Auditions - The King

ThatKiwiGuy December 27, 2019 Voice Acting

Darius is the King and the father of Prince Oscar. He is flamboyant, eccentric and extroverted, but also has a more suave, sly mysterious side. A British accent would suit him well, although the most important aspect of his voice is his two tones, extroverted and happy and suave, sly and seductive.

ThatKiwiGuy · Auditions - The King
  1. It’s going to be absolutely splendid! Son? Ensure that all is in order- Ah! *sudden seductive tone* why, it’s a pleasure to meet you, madam ~
  2. *Sly, cruel* Oh, Reuben it’s an absolute delight to see you~ I was wondering how you were, it’s so nice of you to visit.
  3. *in a hushed voice* but you know that he’s not, he can’t do it! The boy can barely see what’s in front of him, how could he ever rule a Kingdom?
